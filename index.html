Welcome to my Website

Thank you for visiting my site. This site provides some background about me, along with some demonstrations of my work. I put it together to share some of my work (and as an excuse to learn GitHub's site building system).

I am currently a Data Specialist on the Analytics team at Intermedix. We use a variety of analytic tools to extract insights from raw electronic health record data. I previously was a Health Policy Data Analyst at the Vanderbilt University School of Medicine, where I used R, SAS, Python, and Stata to contribute towards numerous research projects.

Please look around, learn more about me, check out my CV, or visit my GitHub page.
